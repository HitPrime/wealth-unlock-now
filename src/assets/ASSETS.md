# Asset Map

Drop your images into the folders below using the exact filenames listed.
Components will import them by these names — wrong filename = broken image.

Accepted formats: `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp` (use `.webp` for best performance)

---

## Kit Item Images
`src/assets/kit/`

These appear on the Pillars/Kit section cards.

| Filename | What it shows |
|---|---|
| `blueprint.png` | The Trader's Blueprint PDF cover |
| `commandments.png` | The Trading Commandments + Glossary bundle (one image covers both) |
| `training-tape.png` | The Training Tape video thumbnail |

Recommended size: 800x600px or 4:3 ratio. Will be cropped to fill the card top area.
